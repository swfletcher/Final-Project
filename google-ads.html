Search Engine Marketing with Google Ads:

More than just a search engine, Google is an advertising network.

Goolge Ads:

Google Ads is how you advertise your business through search engines like Google. If you are researching how to market your business, you have likely seen SEM. This stands for Search Engine Marketing.

Simply put, it is how you get advertisements in front of people looking for something you are offering.

Google has three networks available through Google Ads:

  • Search Network
  • Display Network
  • YouTube Network

Search Network

The search network is where we pay Google for advertisement space at the top of a results page. The ads are labeled as ads, but have an appearance similar to organic results.

These ads are targeted using keywords and search terms. While these results are paid for using PPC (Pay Per Click), quality scores play a role. The quality score is how Google ranks the quality of the landing page the user will land on after they click.

Typically, optimizing a page for SEO will make it an acceptable landing page as well. The better the quality of the page, the less Per Click you will pay.

This is why properly built landing pages are so critical. They can have a dramatic impact on your Google Ads budget.

Google’s Responsive ads are also an amazing addition to the Google Ad arsenal. Gone are the days of making multiple ads with minor differences, hoping to find the winning combination through highest CTR.

Now, one ad can be loaded with multiple headlines and descriptions options. Google’s algorithm will automatically shift spending to the best performers.

Display Network/Gmail

The Google Display Network targets in a similar fashion; keywords and search terms. However, the difference here is rather than appearing as a search result, ads containing images will appear on websites using Google AdSense as a source of website advertising revenue.

Not only, will your image ads appear on websites that are related to the terms you choose, they will also appear on others sites the user visits. When they run a Google search, Google uses that search terms as a point of interest and will display appropriate ads no matter where the user goes.

This also includes Gmail and apps that are running Google Ad Sense.

YouTube Video Network

The YouTube network, or video network, is very simple. You use Google Ads to run video adverts before YouTube content is shown.

The ads are simply videos you have uploaded to your own channel. This means having and maintaining a proper YouTube channel for the organization is a must.
