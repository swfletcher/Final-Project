LinkedIn Advertising:

No questions asked, LinkedIn is the best B2B ad machine.

B2B Advertising with LinkedIn:

LinkedIn Advertising is the most effecting way to promote B2B businesses digitally. Your adverts will appear in the user's newsfeed.

Everytime someone in your target audiece (buyer's persona) logs in to LinkedIn, they could see your ads.

When you complete a LinkedIn profile you are telling digital marketers a lot about yourself. They can use this information to target you for advertising based on:

  • Current employer.
  • Employer size.
  • Employer industry.
  • Education level.
  • Field of study.
  • Job title.
  • Job function.
  • Etc.

The best targeting B2B has ever seen:

The most important targeting characteristic is Member Skills. These are skills the user intentionally added to their profile; they’re not assumptions.

Meaning if you were targeting Sean Fletcher, you would only need to target:

  • Employer: ECKART
  • Skill: HTML

Now apply that to someone else.

Let’s say your company offers professional development for technology companies that employ web developers. Target:

  • Employer: Microsoft
  • Skills: HTML, CSS, etc.

You're with a smaller audience, but the right audience.

Now we have a precise audience. One we know is the right people; Quality over Quantity.

Lead Generation via LinkedIn is a no-brainer:

LinkedIn provides an opportunity to increase conversions. This is because the user can complete and submit a form without ever leaving the platform.

When a user engages with our ads in LinkedIn, a pre-populated form is opened and ready for submission.

The more fields that are added to form, the less likely a user will complete it. However, in B2B there can be a lot of information we need to gather. The LinkedIn Lead Generation tool is so great becuase it eliminates those reductions. Meaning, you can take full advantage of the 12 form fields LinkedIn allows for.

It couldn't be any easier for the user. The only result will be MORE leads!!
